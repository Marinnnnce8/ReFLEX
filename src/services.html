Leasing is managed locally on Orkney, by our experienced electric vehicle delivery partners DriveElectric.

All leases include servicing and a replacement vehicle warranty support (return to mainland) as part of the ReFLEX service package.

Your lease is an FCA regulated agreement. All billing will be managed by our experienced electric vehicle delivery partners DriveElectric.

ReFLEX service package

ReFLEX leases come with the following services included:

  • Easy trade-in for your existing car or van
  • Servicing
  • Replacement vehicles
  • Extended warranty cover
  • Longer-range vehicle use for 14 days a year - No need to worry about your electric car or van range for long-distance trips to other parts of the UK.
